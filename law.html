Environmental laws in India aim to control pollution, conserve natural resources, and ensure sustainable development, reflecting a commitment to fostering a healthier and ecologically balanced future.

Law In Brief Link
The Wildlife Protection Act, 1972 This act is aimed at protecting wildlife and their habitats. It provides for the establishment of wildlife sanctuaries and national parks. Visit
The Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 This act regulates the diversion of forest land for non-forest purposes. It aims to conserve and protect forest areas. Visit
The Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974 This act is focused on preventing and controlling water pollution. It establishes regulatory authorities and standards for water quality. Visit
The Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981 This act deals with the prevention and control of air pollution. It establishes regulatory authorities and emission standards. Visit
The Environment (Protection) Act, 1986 This is a comprehensive law that empowers the central government to take measures to protect and improve the environment. It also provides for the establishment of environmental standards and regulations. Visit
The National Green Tribunal Act, 2010 The National Green Tribunal is a specialized body for the effective and expeditious disposal of cases related to environmental protection and conservation of forests and other natural resources. Visit
The Biological Diversity Act, 2002 This act is aimed at conserving biological diversity in India and promoting sustainable use of its components. It provides for the establishment of a National Biodiversity Authority. Visit
The Hazardous Waste (Management, Handling, and Transboundary Movement) Rules, 2008 These rules provide for the management, handling, and disposal of hazardous waste in an environmentally sound manner. Visit
The Solid Waste Management Rules, 2016 These rules provide a regulatory framework for the management of solid waste, including segregation, collection, and disposal. Visit
The National Environment Appellate Authority Act, 1997 This act provides for the establishment of the National Environment Appellate Authority, which deals with appeals against the orders of the regulatory authorities. Visit
